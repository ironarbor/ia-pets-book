Request a service

Build the quote. Submit the request. Keep control.

This version is intentionally not an instant-book flow. It gives owners an accurate estimate, captures the key service details, and makes clear that payment happens only after you review and approve the request.

Step 1 — Request.

Client chooses dates, service type, dog count, and care details. A live estimate appears before submission.

Step 2 — Review.

You review the request, confirm fit and availability, and can approve as quoted or revise before payment.

Step 3 — Confirm & Pay.

Only approved requests receive a Stripe payment link. Payment locks in the stay and triggers confirmation emails.
